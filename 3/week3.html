Singing Beach User Stories

Kathy, 35, Mother of 2 young kids

Kathy lives in the Boston area and has never been to Singing Beach before. She wants to know:

  • If the beach has parking and if so, whether or not it's close.
  • Are there bathrooms and changing rooms?
  • Is there food for sale nearby?
  • Is the water clean?

Chris, 25, student

Chris is a student living in Boston, he doesn't own a car and he's interested in paddleboarding. He wants to know:

  • Is there a train he can take from Boston? If so, what is the schedule?
  • Is there a place near the beach to rent a paddleboard?
  • Is there a fee to enter the beach?

John, 53, local dog owner

John just moved to Manchester-by-the-Sea and would like to walk his dog on the beach. He wants to know:

  • Are dogs allowed on the beach? If so, are there specific times of year and times of day that they are allowed?
  • Is resident parking available?
  • What are the other resident benefits, if any?
