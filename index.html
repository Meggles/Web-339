Week Ten

Final Project Description

Sailing trips for students around the British Virgin Islands. The website would be a place where students can find information about the boat, captain, the upcoming trip schedule, the trip route and safety information. They will be able to sign up for a trip online and post questions for the captain to answer. There will also be a blog-type page where the captain can post updates about the trip.

User stories

Jen, 28, grad student at Tufts

Jen is considering going on a trip and wants to know:

  • the trip schedule & routes
  • the cost
  • sleeping arrangements
  • what is included in the cost (food, drinks, activities, etc)

Jeff, 31, grad student at Harvard Law

He has already signed up for a trip and wants to know if there’s :

  • cell phone service
  • a shower
  • a way to get to shore early should he feel seasick or claustrophobic
  • what he needs to bring (towels, sheets, food, bugspray, etc)

Mary, 58, Jen’s mom

Mary is Jen’s mom and is worried about her daughter going on the trip. She wants to know:

  • the credentials of the captain
  • details about the boat
  • the trip itinerary
  • emergency procedures
